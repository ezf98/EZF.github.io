Mental Health and Music Survey Project

In this project I cleaned with Python the dataset containing the results of a survey which asked the participants about their favorite music genre and a self reporting of insomnia, anxiety, depression and OCD; on a scale of 0-10. I saved the clean data to a CSV file and then with Power BI made a dashboard, in order to see what were the average ratings per favorite genre, if music improved this conditions, and a general overview of the results (Number of survey takers, age average, hours of music listened per day)
